The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog . This is a test of streaming text layout in egui . We are measuring whether the scroll area jumps or shifts unexpectedly when text is appended token by token . Each token is sent every twenty milliseconds to simulate a language model generating text in real time . The goal is to observe the behavior of egui's immediate mode text rendering under continuous append operations . If the layout is stable the scroll area should smoothly expand downward without jitter or backtracking . Any detected jumps will be logged with timestamps for later analysis .
